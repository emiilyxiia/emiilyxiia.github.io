About Me

I am a first-year student at Columbia University with a major in Computer Science and concentration in Business Management. Fueled by a passion for learning and collaboration, I am interested in building products that can solve real-world problems. While I am most proficient at coding in Java and conducting statistical tests through R, I am always looking to expand my knowledge.


For instance, my creative passions in art and philosophy has led me to self-learn Javascript, HTML, and CSS by hard-coding this website without using any templates or frameworks. I will continue updating it as I learn tools such as React and Bootstrap.


Last but not least, my experience as a game project intern at Tencent has taught me how to optimize user experience and refined my communication skills within a team-based environment. I look forward to continue developing leadership and teamwork skills through contributions to open source, personal/group projects, and internships.
